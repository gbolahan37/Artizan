About Chukwudi

I am a certified plumber with over 9 years of experience serving clients across Lagos Island, Victoria Island, and Lekki. I specialise in residential pipe installation, burst pipe repairs, bathroom plumbing, and drainage systems. I have worked on projects ranging from single-flat repairs to full plumbing installations for residential estates and commercial buildings.

I take pride in arriving on time, explaining the issue clearly before starting work, and cleaning up after every job. All my materials are sourced from trusted suppliers, and I offer a 30-day workmanship guarantee on all repairs. My customers know they can call me back if anything is not right.
