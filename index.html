How Artizan works

01

Search & Discover

Enter your location and the skill you need. See verified artisans on a live map near you, ranked by our SkillScore algorithm.

02

Book & Pay Safely

Choose an artisan, pick an available time slot, describe your job, and pay. Your money is held safely in escrow — not released until you're satisfied.

03

Job Done. You Decide.

The artisan completes your job. You confirm it's done to your satisfaction, then payment is released. Simple, safe, transparent.

Built for safety

Your safety is our
first priority.

We built Artizan because we know how risky it can be to invite a stranger into your home. Every feature — from KYC verification to escrow payment — exists to protect you.

Government ID Verified

Every artisan uploads their NIN, Voter's Card, or Driver's Licence. We verify before they go live.

Escrow Payment Protection

Your money is held securely by Paystack. It's only released when you confirm the job is done right.

TrustScore System

Every artisan has a live TrustScore combining their KYC status, ratings, and completion rate.

Dispute Resolution

If anything goes wrong, our team steps in. You can raise a dispute and we'll investigate fairly.
