1. About Artizan

Artizan is an online marketplace that connects customers in Lagos, Nigeria with verified skilled artisans offering home, personal, and professional services. Artizan is a platform — we are not the employer of any artisan and we do not provide the services ourselves. We facilitate discovery, booking, communication, and secure payment between independent parties.

2. Eligibility

To use Artizan you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Be a resident of or operating within Nigeria
  • Provide accurate and truthful registration information
  • Have the legal capacity to enter into a binding contract under Nigerian law

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts where eligibility requirements are not met.

3. Customer obligations

As a customer on Artizan, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate job descriptions and location details when making a booking
  • Be available or accessible to the artisan at the agreed date and time
  • Make payment only through the Artizan platform — not directly to the artisan before job completion
  • Release escrowed funds promptly after confirming a job is complete
  • Leave honest, fair reviews based on your genuine experience
  • Not attempt to solicit artisans to work off-platform to avoid Artizan fees

4. Artisan obligations

As an artisan on Artizan, you agree to:

  • Complete KYC identity verification using a valid Nigerian government-issued ID
  • Only offer services in skill categories in which you are genuinely qualified
  • Respond to booking requests within 24 hours
  • Arrive at the job location at the agreed time or communicate delays promptly
  • Complete jobs to a professional standard and as described in the booking
  • Not solicit additional payments from customers outside the agreed and escrowed price
  • Keep your availability status, portfolio, and pricing information accurate

5. Bookings and the escrow payment system

All payments on Artizan are processed through Paystack and held in escrow until a job is confirmed complete:

  • Step 1 — Booking request: The customer submits a job description and their proposed budget.
  • Step 2 — Negotiation: The artisan may accept the price or send a counter-offer. Both parties negotiate until a price is agreed.
  • Step 3 — Escrow payment: Once the price is agreed, the customer pays via Paystack. Funds are held by Artizan in escrow — the artisan does not receive payment yet.
  • Step 4 — Job completion: The artisan marks the job done and may upload a proof photo.
  • Step 5 — Payment release: The customer confirms the job is complete. Artizan releases the escrowed funds to the artisan within 1–2 business days.

Artizan charges a platform fee of 8% of the agreed job price, deducted from the artisan's payout. This fee covers payment processing, KYC verification, and platform maintenance.

6. Price negotiation

Artizan's negotiation feature allows customers and artisans to agree a fair price before any payment is made:

  • Either party may send a counter-offer. There is no limit on the number of rounds of negotiation.
  • A negotiation is binding only when both parties have confirmed the agreed price and the customer has completed escrow payment.
  • Artisans may not request additional payments beyond the agreed and escrowed price once a job has started.
  • Customers may not attempt to reduce the agreed price after job completion.

7. Cancellations and refunds

  • Before escrow payment: Either party may cancel at any time with no charge.
  • After escrow payment, before job start: A full refund is issued to the customer within 3–5 business days if the artisan cancels or does not show up.
  • After job start: Refunds are subject to dispute resolution (see Section 8).
  • Customer no-show: If the customer is unreachable at the agreed time, the artisan may cancel and is entitled to a cancellation fee of 20% of the agreed price, deducted from the refund.

8. Disputes

If a customer and artisan cannot resolve a disagreement, either party may open a dispute through the platform. Artizan will:

  • Review the booking details, chat history, and any uploaded proof photos
  • Contact both parties to hear their account of events
  • Issue a resolution within 5 business days
  • Artizan's decision on disputes is final within the platform, but does not preclude either party from seeking legal remedies under Nigerian law

Violations may result in immediate account suspension, forfeiture of escrowed funds where applicable, and referral to law enforcement.
